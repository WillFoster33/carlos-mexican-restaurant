🌵 Our Story

Bringing the Rich Flavors of Mexico to Westland

Carlos Mexican Restaurant is a family-owned gem serving Westland and the surrounding communities. Every dish is made with love, fresh ingredients, and authentic recipes passed down through generations.

From sizzling fajitas to our famous birria tacos, we bring the bold, homemade flavors of Mexico right to your table -no shortcuts, no compromises. Whether you're dining in with family or grabbing carry out for the night, you're always welcome here.
